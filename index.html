About

My name is Jasper De landsheere, I'm a Belgian M.Sc. student at Ghent University. Currently, I am doing my Master's thesis on predicting pharmaceutical granules' size using Kernel Mean Embedding. This thesis is hosted in the department of data analysis and mathematical modelling at the faculty of Bioscience Engineering, specifically under the research groups KERMIT (Knowledge-based, predictive and spatio-temporal modelling) and BIOMATH (Advanced process modelling). My thesis promotors are Dr. Ir. Michiel Stock and Dr. Ir. Daan Van Hauwermeiren.
